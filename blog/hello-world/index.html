Hello World!

07.18.2015 1 minute

Not having a live portfolio for someone in my industry is like shooting yourself in the foot, and neglecting to have one for over 2 years - well, its like not having feet. Disturbing, and quite difficult to get around.

I’d like to blame it on my busy schedule, an abundance of work, a social life, or even the perfectionist in me not being happy with any of the versions I attempted to build, but honestly - I was lazy. The thought of having to not only design and develop a portfolio, but keeping it up to date with in depth case studies and constantly writing blog posts seemed daunting and cumbersomb.

Its not that I dont have the material, rather, getting set up mentally, emotionally and physically to write something meaningful is rather difficult and a big investment of time. I wish there was a way to write these articles while im on the john, or in the shower - since that’s when my mind is on overdrive and I could kill two birds with one stone. ( Note: no animals were harmed in the making of this article ).

So Im rather proud of myself for finally launching my site, although long overdue, atleast its a step - yeah I got my feet back. And with this launch, im striving for daily posts - however small - as long as its daily, eventually I’ll become a better writer and posting will no longer be a drag - and honestly, hopefully someone out there will benefit from my random thoughts.

Stay classy San Diego, or should I say the Internetz.
